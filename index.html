Palestina Posters NL
This is a collection of 97 Dutch posters about Palestina, grabbed* from Het Geheugen, most particularly from the Afficheproject. The posters, containing text in Dutch, Arabic, Turkish and a bit of English, relate to Dutch activism and advocacy for the Palestinian struggle in different ways. Some of them are bold visual statements in themselves, proclaiming a liberatory message. Others share information about the Palestinian context. And a large part of the collection is posters for events like demonstrations, lectures, cultural events, etc.

I made a loose curation of the different posters, connecting them by topic, phrasing, aesthetic, context and language. Besides putting them in a pleasing and thoughtful order, I didn't make a selection from the website materials - I just took out doubles, and didn't include materials specifically related to Libanon. I recommend using Google Translate's Lens to get translations of the texts.

If you click** on the images, you will be taken to the Geheugen website which contains more extensive information about the posters. Most noticably, only the designs made by professional white male designers are properly credited.
